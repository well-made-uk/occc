Topics covered:

What is carbon footprint?

What are greenhouse gasses and how are they created? What happens when they accumulate in our atmosphere?

What is the carbon footprint of individuals and common items?

What is the carbon footprint of food? What is the carbon footprint of individuals living in different countries? What has a higher carbon footprint? (ex. wired vs wireless headphones, plastic or glass jar, etc)

How do we determine the carbon footprint of items?

What is a lifecycle analysis (LCA)? What is a carbon label? How are LCAs being used in the industry?

How do we determine the carbon footprint of items?

What is the historical concentration of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere? What is the concentration now and what are the key inflection points?
